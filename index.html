About Me

During my bachelor's degree in Videogames, I've worked on multiple projects, both individual and as part of a team. In this time, I've developed basic and advanced skills in various tools for the production of multimedia applications.

As part of my degree, I interned at a secondary school, where I assisted students during their videogame projects in a Computer Applications class, by creating simple prototypes and giving them tips on how to better implement their ideas. Besides that, I developed a game to be featured in the school's webpage, based around the history of the institution's patron.

I am always looking for opportunities to improve as a developer.
